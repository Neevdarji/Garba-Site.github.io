About Us

Introduction

Welcome To Disha Garba Classess, Where We Celebrate The Vibrant Tradition Of Garba And Bring The Joy Of Dance To All Ages. Established In 2015, We Are Dedicated To Promoting The Rich Cultural Heritage Of Garba And Fostering A Sense Of Community Through Dance.

Our Mission

At Disha Garba Classess, our mission is to:

Preserve Tradition: We aim to preserve and promote the traditional art of Garba, ensuring that this beautiful cultural dance form continues to thrive for generations to come.

Share the Joy: We believe that dance is not just a form of exercise; it's a celebration of life. Our classes are designed to spread happiness and create unforgettable memories on the dance floor.

Build Community: We are committed to building a close-knit community of Garba enthusiasts who support and inspire each other on their dance journey.
